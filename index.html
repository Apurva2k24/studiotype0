About

Amazon is a multinational technology company that focuses on e-commerce, cloud computing, and artificial intelligence. The company has also expanded into new areas such as cloud computing with Amazon Web Services (AWS) and artificial intelligence with Alexa, a virtual assistant integrated into various devices. Amazon's success has led to significant growth, making it one of the world's most valuable companies.
